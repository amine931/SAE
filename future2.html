World Manuals Teaching History Across Cultures

From March 28 to October 5, 2026

Download the Program

Presentation of the event

The 2026 edition expands on the previous exhibition by offering an immersion into history teaching around the world. Through some 100 textbooks from various countries and eras, the exhibition explains how political, social, and educational issues influence this content.

Discover how history is taught in over 50 countries, from the 17th century to the present day, and compare different cultural perspectives on major historical events.

Themes of the Exhibition

Colonization and decolonization

How these periods are taught across countries and eras. Discover the different perspectives on colonial expansion and independence movements.

Explorer

The World Wars

European, Asian, and American perspectives on these major conflicts. Analyze how different nations recount these traumatic events.

Explorer

History in the Digital Age

Presenting digital projects modernizing history teaching. Discover how new technologies are transforming our relationship with the past.

Explorer

Propaganda or education?

Analysis of textbooks under authoritarian and democratic regimes. Understand how ideology shapes the teaching of history.
